I was born and raised in Germany and I joined the Army when I was 17. While in the military I served as a Rigger in a Special Forces unit. I developed and tested new types of equipment including bomb blankets and body armor. I also trained and operated extensivly in close quarters combat. While not working, training, or on deployment, I would spend my time instructing civilian, military, and law enforcement through a local academy. When I was 27 I got out of the military started a mixed martial arts and personal training company. A couple years ago I moved to Alexandria, Va and have been working on getting a degree in Biomedical Engineering. I am also curently studying Front-end web developement in my free time.

What do you want to do when you grow up?

I don’t have a dream job, I just want to be able to have the freedom to do pretty much whatever I want most of the time. I really enjoy traveling and moving around I really want to do something that I can take with me or that is worthwhile enough to keep up with once I finally decide to settle down. My goal is to put in enough work and find the right direction in order to make a worthwile living as a freelance web developer.
